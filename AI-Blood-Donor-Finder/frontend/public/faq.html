Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How do I register as a donor?

You can register by clicking "Register" in the top menu and filling out the donor registration form. It takes less than 2 minutes to complete. You'll need to provide your basic information, blood group, location, and contact details.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) and JWT authentication to protect your data. We never share your personal information without your explicit consent, except in emergency situations where a donor match is needed.

How quickly can I find a blood donor?

Our AI system typically finds compatible donors within 2-5 minutes. Emergency alerts reach nearby donors within seconds. The actual response time depends on donor availability in your area, but most requests are fulfilled within 15-30 minutes.

What blood groups are available?

We support all standard blood groups: A+, A-, B+, B-, AB+, AB-, O+, and O-. Our AI matching system considers blood group compatibility, location, and donor availability to find the best matches.

Can I use the voice assistant?

Yes! Our voice assistant is available on all pages. Click the microphone button at the bottom left of any page to activate it. You can use voice commands like "Find O+ blood in Mumbai" or "Send emergency alert" to navigate the platform hands-free.

How does the AI fake request detection work?

Our AI system analyzes request patterns, user behavior, and historical data to identify potentially fake requests. It checks for unusual patterns like repeated requests from the same IP, inconsistent information, and suspicious timing. Suspicious requests are flagged for manual review.

Can hospitals use this platform?

Yes! Hospitals can register for a hospital account and access our hospital dashboard. The dashboard provides blood inventory tracking, demand forecasting, donor management, and analytics to help manage blood supplies more effectively.

What are the eligibility criteria for blood donation?

Donors must be at least 18 years old, weigh at least 50 kg, and be in good health. There are some medical conditions that may temporarily or permanently disqualify you from donating. Our registration form includes health screening questions to ensure eligibility.

How often can I donate blood?

The recommended interval between blood donations is at least 56 days (8 weeks) for whole blood donations. This allows your body sufficient time to replenish the blood cells. Our system automatically tracks your donation history and will notify you when you're eligible to donate again.

Is there a mobile app available?

We're currently developing mobile apps for both iOS and Android. The web application is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. You can also install our Progressive Web App (PWA) on your phone for a native app-like experience.
