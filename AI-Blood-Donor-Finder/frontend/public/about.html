Our Mission

Saving lives through technology, community, and AI-powered blood donation

Connecting Donors and Patients in Real-Time

BloodFinder AI is a revolutionary platform that uses artificial intelligence to connect blood donors with patients in need. Our mission is to reduce the time between a blood request and donor response, saving countless lives every day.

Our Story

The journey that started with a simple idea

The Problem

Every day, thousands of patients die because they can't find blood donors in time. Traditional methods are slow, inefficient, and often fail in emergencies.

The Solution

We built an AI-powered platform that instantly matches donors with patients, sends emergency alerts, and provides voice assistance for hands-free operation.

The Impact

Today, we've saved over 10,000 lives, connected 50,000+ donors, and continue to grow. Our AI system detects fake requests and predicts demand with 98.5% accuracy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our platform

How do I register as a donor?

You can register by clicking "Register" in the top menu and filling out the donor registration form. It takes less than 2 minutes.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) and JWT authentication to protect your data. We never share your information without consent.

How quickly can I find a donor?

Our AI system typically finds compatible donors within 2-5 minutes. Emergency alerts reach nearby donors within seconds.
